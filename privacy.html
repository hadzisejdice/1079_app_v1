Privacy & GDPR

No personal data collected: 1079Magic does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations are performed locally in your browser.

No cookies: This tool does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

No analytics: We do not use any third-party analytics services.

Local processing: All troop data, statistics, and calculations remain entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

OCR processing: If you use the screenshot import feature, image processing is done locally in your browser using Tesseract.js. No images are uploaded to any server.

Third-party resources: This site loads the Plotly.js library from a CDN for chart rendering. Please refer to Plotly's privacy policy for their CDN data handling.

GDPR compliance: Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to request, modify, or delete. You can use this tool with confidence that your privacy is fully protected.

Contact: For any privacy concerns, please reach out via the Patreon page.
