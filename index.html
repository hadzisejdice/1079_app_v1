📊 How to collect troop stats:
  1. 🎯 Start a rally on Terror using your strongest heroes (same as for Bear), at full capacity.
  2. 📄 Open your battle reports → find the Terror rally → take a screenshot of the Bonus Details section and import it
    OR manually enter:
    Infantry / Cavalry / Archers – Attack% & Lethality%.
  3. 🪖 Open your in‑game Squad screen → screenshot troop amounts → import OR manually type the troop numbers below.
  4. 📐 Go to Squad → Formation to check your rally size.
  5. 📸 Use the "Import stats from screenshot" button OR enter all values manually.
