Bear formation optimizer for Kingshot — Magic Ratio & Option-A

1 Start a rally on Terror using your strongest heroes (same as for Bear), at full capacity.
2 Open battle reports → find the Terror rally → screenshot the Bonus Details section and import, or manually enter Infantry / Cavalry / Archers – Attack% & Lethality%.
3 Open your in-game Squad screen → screenshot troop amounts → import or manually type the troop numbers below.
4 Go to Squad → Formation to check your rally size.
5 Use the "Import from screenshot" button or enter all values manually.

    Tests all formation combinations to find the best 10 call/join ratios with highest total score.

    • Runs damage simulation on all possible ratios
    • Calculates best 10 combinations
    • Shows scoreboard sorted by total damage
    • Recommends optimal march count
    • Auto-fills your call rally & join formations

    FOCUS mode Reduces archers in CALL rally to maximize JOIN rally archer count (you join 2× per 5-min window = 2× damage!)
